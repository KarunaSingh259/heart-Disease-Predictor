Echocardiogram Models (Tab 4)
R2Plus1D-18 (3D CNN) 84.3%

The R2Plus1D-18 architecture is a state-of-the-art 3D Convolutional Neural Network designed for video classification. It decomposes 3D convolutions into separate spatial (2D) and temporal (1D) components, allowing for more efficient learning of spatiotemporal features in echocardiogram sequences.
