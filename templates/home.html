About the Project

Cardio AI is an advanced, multimodal prediction system designed to assist healthcare professionals and individuals in identifying the risk of heart disease.

This system utilizes state-of-the-art AI models to predict heart disease by analyzing diverse data sources, including:

Clinical Data
ECG Images
Echo Scans
Medical Reports

By combining multiple Artificial Intelligence models, Cardio AI aims to improve prediction quality, reduce diagnostic errors, and provide actionable user support.

Key Features

Clinical Prediction

Input 11 clinical parameters to get an instant risk assessment.

Bulk Prediction

Upload a CSV file for screening multiple patients at once.

ECG Analysis

Deep learning based classification of 12-lead ECG images.

Echo Analysis

Detect abnormalities using echocardiogram videos or images.

Report Summary

Extract insights and combine predictions from medical reports.

Nearby Hospitals

Locate specialized heart care facilities in your vicinity.

How It Works
  • 1 Enter patient details or upload data (CSV, Image, PDF).
  • 2 AI model processes the input securely.
  • 3 Prediction is generated instantly.
  • 4 Explanation and recommendation are shown.
  • 5 Nearby hospitals are suggested if needed.
